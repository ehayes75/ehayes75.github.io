About

My name is Emma Hayes. I am a current Mathemtics PhD student at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. I completed my undergraduate studies at Carnegie Mellon University. My mathematical interests lie in applied math in general but specifically differential equations, and I love to incorporate computer science or machine learning in any projects I do.
I love being able to explore the relationships between math and computer science and work with real life problems and examples. Outside of math I like to bake, hike, and make art.
