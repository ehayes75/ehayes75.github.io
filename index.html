About

My name is Emma Hayes. I am a current senior at Carnegie Mellon University studying Computational and Applied Mathematics with a minor in Computer Science. My mathematical interests lie in applied math in general but specifically differential equations, and I would love to incorporate computer science or machine learning in any projects I do.
